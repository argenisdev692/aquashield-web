AquaShield Restoration LLC - Website Design Brief
Project Overview
Create a modern, clean, and mobile-friendly website for AquaShield Restoration LLC, a water damage restoration and protection company.
Brand Identity
Company Name
AquaShield Restoration LLC.

Tagline: "Total Restoration & Protection"
Note: Maintain capital letters in "Aqua" and "Shield" for visual emphasis on protection

Logo Elements

Shield: Main symbol representing protection and security
House: Integrated or silhouetted within the design to identify the construction and remodeling sector
Wave: Stylized to represent water damage mitigation specialty ("Aqua")

Color Palette
Primary Colors

Navy Blue (Deep Marine Blue): #0C2340

RGB: rgb(12, 35, 64)
CMYK: C:100 M:76 Y:12 K:70
Usage: Main brand color, text, exterior shield, buttons, headers
Conveys: Authority, trust, and experience


Aqua Blue (Bright Aqua): #00B5E2

RGB: rgb(0, 181, 226)
CMYK: C:70 M:0 Y:15 K:0
Usage: Secondary color, the "wave", water details, accents, hover states
Conveys: Freshness and action



Supporting Colors

Pure White: #FFFFFF

RGB: rgb(255, 255, 255)
Usage: Backgrounds, text on dark navy


Light Silver Gray: #E6E7E8

RGB: rgb(230, 231, 232)
Usage: Subtle metallic details (optional), section dividers



Typography

Font Family: Roboto (Sans Serif)
Alternatives: Montserrat for a modern look
Font Weights:

Light (300) for body text
Regular (400) for general content
Medium (500) for emphasis
Bold (700) for headings



Design Requirements
Layout Style

Clean and modern design
Easy mobile navigation
Responsive design for all devices
Professional and trustworthy aesthetic
Clear visual hierarchy
